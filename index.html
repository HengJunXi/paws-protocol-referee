Referee Rules

Cards & ranks

Resolving an attack

  • Higher rank eliminates lower rank.
  • Equal rank (same card) → both eliminated.
  • Lion vs Lion → the attacker wins.
  • Rat vs Lion, Mine or Missile → Rat always wins.
  • Missile attacking destroys the target but self-destructs (both eliminated); when attacked it is destroyed — except by a Mine, which it survives. Beaten by a Rat.
  • Mine attacking always self-destructs and the defender survives (two Mines eliminate each other); when attacked, it destroys its attacker but self-destructs (both eliminated). Beaten by a Rat.
  • Furor Tigris → Colonel Tiger strikes two targets, each resolved separately; Tiger is eliminated if it loses either. In 1v1 the targets must differ; in 2v2 they may be the same card.
  • Lionheart → each activated General Lion gives that side's units +1 rank (up to +1 in 1v1, +2 in 2v2 with both Lions). Applies to the rank comparison only.

Winning

  • 1v1: the game ends the instant a General Lion is eliminated — whoever eliminated it wins.
  • 2v2: a team is defeated only when both of its General Lions are eliminated; losing just one keeps the game going.
This referee only reports survived / eliminated — it never reveals a card's identity. Furor Tigris and Lionheart are handled here; other Unique Skills (Grizzly Guard, etc.) are applied by the players themselves.
