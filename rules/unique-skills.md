# Protocol Directives — Unique Skills

There are 9 cards in total. The 7 **character** cards below have Unique Skills.
The 2 **weaponry** cards (Mini Missile, Micro Mine) have **no** Unique Skills.

| Card (Character) | Rank (stars) | Skill Name |
|------------------|:------------:|------------|
| General Lion     | ★★★★★★★ (7) | LIONHEART |
| Colonel Tiger    | ★★★★★★ (6)  | FUROR TIGRIS |
| Major Bear       | ★★★★★ (5)   | GRIZZLY GUARD |
| Captain Boar     | ★★★★ (4)    | TUSK RAMPAGE |
| Lieutenant Wolf  | ★★★ (3)     | LUNAR INSTINCT |
| Sergeant Cat     | ★★ (2)      | PURRFECT COVER |
| Commando Rat     | ★ (1)       | (RE)MOVE THAT CHEESE |

## Skill Descriptions

### LIONHEART — General Lion (★★★★★★★)
All ally units gain +1 rank until the end of your next turn.

### FUROR TIGRIS — Colonel Tiger (★★★★★★)
Attacks two different targets simultaneously this turn. Both attacks must be resolved.

### GRIZZLY GUARD — Major Bear (★★★★★)
Activate only when an ally unit is targeted by an attack. Redirect that attack to Major Bear instead.

### TUSK RAMPAGE — Captain Boar (★★★★)
Expose one opponent card of your choice. Unique Skill of the card will not be activated.

### LUNAR INSTINCT — Lieutenant Wolf (★★★)
Opponent chooses to expose a Mine or Missile.

### PURRFECT COVER — Sergeant Cat (★★)
Hide an exposed card. A rehidden card can be activated again.

### (RE)MOVE THAT CHEESE — Commando Rat (★)
Redeploy an eliminated Mine or Missile in any position, without revealing their identity.

---

**Note:** Weaponry cards (Mini Missile 🚀, Micro Mine 💣) have no Unique Skills.

> The referee web app (`index.html`) resolves raw card-vs-card combat and additionally
> supports two Unique Skills: **Furor Tigris** (Colonel Tiger attacks two targets) and
> **Lionheart** (a per-side +1-rank toggle for each player). All other Unique Skills are
> applied manually by the players during play.
